What We Do

About Us

We are a Montreal-based company with a team full of people who have a passion for food and travel. Join us on our journey in search of delicious food stops along unforgettable road trip routes through our "A La Carte" web application.

Find Good Food

We find great locations for you to stop at along your journey whether it be a restaurant, coffee shop, bar or small town diner. Take a quick break to grab a bite before you hit the road again.

Promote Local Businesses

We want to inspire our users to travel and find local places that they would not have known about. There are hidden food treasures along your route that are waiting to be discovered!

How It Works

  • Step 1

    Choose Point A and Point B

    Input your origin (Point A) and your destination (Point B) then click search to find the route for your journey.

  • Step 2

    Discover Stops Along Your Route

    Once you click search, you will be brought to the results page where you can find all the restaurants that are along your route.

  • Step 3

    Explore and Eat

    Pick your favourite restaurants along the way and make frequent stops to eat!
